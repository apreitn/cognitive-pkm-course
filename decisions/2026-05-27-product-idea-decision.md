# Product Idea Decision

## Decision
I decided to develop a Service Desk Ticket Helper.

## Reason
This idea is practical and connected to real IT support work. Many support requests are unclear, so a tool that helps create structured tickets can save time and improve communication.

## Alternatives considered
- Simple task manager
- Study planner
- Knowledge base website

## Why this option was selected
The Service Desk Ticket Helper has a clear problem, clear users, and realistic features that can be developed during the course.
